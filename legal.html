Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

Who we are

Company Architect is a free browser-based business simulation game developed and operated by Charlie Leach. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights regarding that data.

If you have any questions, contact us at: companyarchitectsupport@gmail.com

What data we collect

We only collect data if you choose to create an account. Guest players are not tracked in any way.

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to identify your account
  • Display name — shown on the public leaderboard
  • Game save data — your turn number, money, facilities, products, and other in-game state so you can continue where you left off
  • Leaderboard data — your display name, prestige level, and highest balance, shown publicly

We do not collect your real name, address, phone number, or payment information.

How we use your data

  • To save your game progress so you can resume on any device
  • To display your score on the public leaderboard
  • To identify your account when you sign in

We do not sell your data, share it with third parties for marketing, or use it for advertising profiling.

Who stores your data

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), operated by Google LLC. Firebase is hosted in the European Union. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

If you sign in with Google, Google's own authentication service handles your login. We only receive your email address and display name from Google.

Your rights under UK GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — you can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen inside the game
  • Right to rectification — you can update your display name by contacting us
  • Right to object — you can stop using the game and delete your account at any time

To exercise any of these rights, use the Delete Account button in the game's Settings screen, or email us at companyarchitectsupport@gmail.com.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies or any third-party tracking or analytics tools. Firebase may store a session token in your browser's local storage to keep you signed in between visits. This is not a tracking cookie and is not shared with any third party.

Children

Company Architect is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not create an account. If we become aware that a user under 13 has registered, we will delete their account and data.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
