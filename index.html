💡 Tips

Buy facilities early — no employees means no sales capacity and no revenue.
Hire before you end a turn — employees drive your sales capacity every turn.
Launch products straight away — you earn nothing until a product is live.
Price close to the market price — going too high kills demand even with high popularity.
Invest early — investments run for multiple turns and compound over time.
R&D is permanent — Supply Chain Deal and Automation Systems give the best long-term value.
Prestige when you hit £1M — your starting money multiplier stacks every time.
Seasons affect sales — Food booms in Summer, Clothing in Winter. Plan your production.
Loans are useful early — take a small loan to buy your second facility faster.
Products age — popularity slowly drops after a few turns. Launch new ones to keep revenue up.
Reputation matters — keep it above 60 for a sales boost. It drops when you fire staff.
Food tip — price your items at 2.5x production cost for the best balance of margin and demand.
